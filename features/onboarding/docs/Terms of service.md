### 3. Key Features

Mangala enables users to:

- Create/import non-custodial wallets
- Send/receive tokens across supported blockchains
- Buy, sell, and swap crypto assets
- Stake tokens on supported chains
- Use a built-in **AI assistant** (requires passkey login)
- Recover wallets securely from private keys or seed phrases

The App integrates select third-party services such as Infura, Chainlink, and CoinGecko for real-time data and blockchain interaction.
